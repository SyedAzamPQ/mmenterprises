about us

Welcome to MM Enterprises

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as Interior Contractors and Furnishers. Briefly we are a Bangalore based company having vast experience in the execution of prestigious interior works for various corporate Groups during the last 26 years, involved in various fields of Interior works ranging from Hotel Projects, Restaurants, Software Offices, Commercial Office and showroom interiors in addition to Residential interior works.

We at MM enterprises believe that each project is as unique as each client. The core function of our company is Interiors Decoration, Common area finishes and Civil work as its implementation along with Architecture as a collaborative process.
